  • comparedInfo : A and B's processed info : name, code, begin, end, low, high, ROD, close, logYield, logYieldVariance

  • marketInfo : volume 、 涨（跌）停股票数、涨（跌）幅超过5% 的股票数、开盘‐收盘大（小）于 5%*上一个交易日收盘价的股票个数
